Branding

Company

Ano Sushi is committed to serving quality sushi in the lower mainland. Our restaurants feature conveyer belt styled self service—perfect for the casual yet exciting dining experience.

Customers

The target audience of our restaurants are young millennials looking for a convient dinning experience. These customers enjoy using technology to streamline their experience. Whether that may be picking up or making reservations through the restaurant's app. Additionally, these customers are enticed by unique dining experiences like the self-service conveyer belt style we offer. These options of fast casual dining mesh well with this generation's preferences. They care about locally and sustainable food. But over all else, they love sushi.

Fonts

The primary typeface of Ano Sushi is Cabin. Headings use 400 weight. Paragraphs use 300 weight.

Colors

Primary #8aec6f
Secondary #ef832b
Gray 500 #808080

Art Direction

Ano Sushi's art direction features flat design with clear and bold type hierarchy. The colours and styling embodies a causal and modern tone — well fitted to the casual dinning experience of Ano Sushi. Shadows are soft and corners are rounded. Photography features brightly lit, uplifting images.

Ant Design Icon Set was chosen for its flat and minimal iconography.
